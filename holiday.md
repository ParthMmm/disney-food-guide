Time to get festive (and hungry) because **[Holidays at the Disneyland Resort](https://disneyland.disney.go.com/events-tours/holidays-at-the-disneyland-resort/?CMP=ILC-DPFY25Q4wo1030250027G)** is nearly here! And you know what that means — beginning Nov. 14, 2025, through Jan. 7, 2026, you’ll be able to get plenty of seasonal treats to get you into the holiday spirit.

There’s snow much to savor this year — a true sleigh-full of goodies! From returning fan favorites like the **Celebration Dinner Potato from Troubadour Tavern** and **Chicken Pot Pie Soup from Aunt Cass Café** to all-new yuletide delights such as the **Gingerbread-Cranberry Cheesecake Trifle from Jolly Holiday Bakery Cafe** and **Apple Bundt Cake from Plaza Inn**, your taste buds will need to prepare for a blizzard of deliciousness.

![](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=z13Rat08jU4)

At Disney California Adventure Park, you can also celebrate with [**Disney Festival of Holidays**](https://disneyland.disney.go.com/events-tours/disney-california-adventure/festival-of-holidays/) from November 14, 2025, through January 7, 2026, featuring a variety of flavorful food and beverages from a season of diverse celebrations [Disney Festival of Holidays Foodie Guide](https://disneyparksblog.com/dlr/disney-eats-disney-festival-of-holidays-at-disneyland-foodie-guide-2025/).

Now, let’s jump right in and unwrap all the season has to offer!
